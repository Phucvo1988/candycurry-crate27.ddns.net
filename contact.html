Frequently Asked Questions

What is candy-curry fusion cuisine?

Candy-curry fusion is an innovative culinary approach that combines the sweet flavors of candy and desserts with the aromatic spices of traditional curry dishes. This creates unique flavor profiles that surprise and delight the palate.

Are your recipes suitable for vegetarians?

Yes! We offer a wide variety of vegetarian and vegan recipes. Each recipe clearly indicates dietary information, and we often provide alternative ingredients to accommodate different dietary needs.

Can I submit my own candy-curry fusion recipe?

Absolutely! We love featuring recipes from our community. You can submit your recipe through our contact form, and our team will review it for possible inclusion on our website or social media channels.

Do you offer cooking classes or workshops?

We periodically host virtual cooking classes and workshops. Please check our events page or subscribe to our newsletter to stay updated on upcoming sessions.

Where can I find specialty ingredients for your recipes?

Most of our recipes use common ingredients available at regular grocery stores. For more unique items, we provide links to online retailers where you can purchase them. We also often suggest suitable substitutions.
